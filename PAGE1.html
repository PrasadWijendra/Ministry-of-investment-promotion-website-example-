Our service

AutoMobile:

The Ministry of Investment Promotion facilitates investments in the automobile sector, promoting advancements in manufacturing, technology, and sustainable practices. We aim to attract investments that enhance production capacities, innovate in electric and autonomous vehicles, and contribute to economic growth.

Healthcare:

Our ministry supports investments in healthcare to improve access to quality healthcare services, develop medical infrastructure, and advance research and development. We focus on attracting investments in hospitals, pharmaceuticals, medical devices, and digital health solutions, aiming to enhance public health outcomes and economic development.

Construction:

The Ministry of Investment Promotion encourages investments in construction to drive infrastructure development, urban planning, and sustainable building solutions. We aim to attract investments that enhance transportation networks, residential and commercial developments, and green building initiatives, fostering economic growth and improving quality of life.
